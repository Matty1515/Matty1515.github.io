Autumn Nations Cup


England National Team at the Autumn Nations Cup


Due to the travel restrictions caused by the COVID-19 pandemic, the normal Autumn International games have been replaced by a new Autumn Nations Cup. All Six Nations teams will participate, plus Fiji and Georgia, across 4 game-weeks in November and December 2020.

Teams will first play against every other team in their group once to determine their group standings. Then top team in Group A will face the top team in Group B, same for the second, third and fourth place teams in their groups determining the overall Nations Cup standings. The nation that wins their group and beats the opposing group leader will win the Autumn Nations Cup.
